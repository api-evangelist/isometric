Isometric announced $40 million in Series A financing, led by AVP (with insurer AXA as anchor investor) and joined by Plural, Lowercarbon Capital, John Doerr, and Walter Kortschak, to expand its agentic certification across the industrial economy. Its AI-powered Certify platform uses agents to cross-check millions of data points from sensors, satellite imagery, supply chain records, and lab results, cutting certification timelines from 12+ months to hours. Isometric is the largest carbon removal certifier with over 16 million tonnes contracted, serving Microsoft, Anglo American, JPMorganChase, and Boeing.
